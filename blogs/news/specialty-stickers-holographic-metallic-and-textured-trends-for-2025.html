What Are Specialty Stickers?

Specialty stickers use unique materials and finishes to create eye-catching effects. In 2025, demand for holographic and metallic stickers is up 30% year-over-year.

What Types of Specialty Stickers Are Trending?

  • Holographic: Rainbow shimmer that changes with the light.

  • Metallic: Gold, silver, and foil for a luxury look.

  • Textured: Raised, embossed, or glitter finishes for tactile appeal.

Where Are Specialty Stickers Used?

  • Branding: Make your logo unforgettable.

  • Events: Limited edition swag and collectibles.

  • Packaging: Add a premium touch to products.

Are Specialty Stickers Durable?

Yes modern specialty stickers are waterproof, UV-resistant, and designed to last indoors or outdoors.
